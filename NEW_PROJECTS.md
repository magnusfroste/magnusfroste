Your dedicated bookkeeping assistant that automates financial tracking, data parsing, and reporting with seamless integration for Swedish standards. Talk to, write, or upload receipts and invoices - the AI assistant automatically detects and proposes how to bookkeep.

### Key Features
- **Natural Language Input**: Talk or write to the assistant in plain language
- **Document Upload**: Upload receipts and invoices for automatic processing

- **Proposal System**: AI suggests how to bookkeep before you confirm

### Impact
Revolutionizing bookkeeping for Swedish businesses, making financial management accessible and efficient for everyone from freelancers to small businesses.
